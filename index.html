About Me

A Few Words About Me

Hi, my name is Vinh! I was born on January 9th, 2005, and I’m from Quang Ngai, Vietnam. I’m currently a student at Duy Tan University in Da Nang.

Ever since I was a kid, I’ve been fascinated by technology and electronics. In my free time, I love diving into how things work, researching new tech, and exploring gadgets. That curiosity has helped me build up a bit of knowledge and hands-on experience over time.

I have a strong passion for exploring digital systems, uncovering tricks and bugs in apps and websites, and even “cracking” applications to understand how they work under the hood. When I’m not busy bug hunting, I enjoy working on various tech projects and sharing what I’ve learned through online platforms and communities I’m part of.

A Turning Point in Life

A turning point came during my high school years, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic when I had more time at home. That was when I began teaching myself web development and exploring everything that sparked my curiosity — from creating simple websites to tinkering with smartphones, such as rooting devices and installing custom ROMs on Android.

I was lucky to be guided and supported by many experienced mentors who helped me understand even the most basic concepts. Thanks to them, I learned a lot from both successes and mistakes. I didn’t just stick to theory – I also built a few small websites as milestones to mark my personal growth.

Hobbies

Besides programming, I have quite a few other interests:

  • I love playing sports, especially soccer with friends – it’s a great way to relax and stay fit.
  • I enjoy exploring new technology and discovering cool features or innovations I haven’t seen before.
  • I’m always happy to meet new people and have meaningful conversations, which helps me broaden my horizons and learn from others’ life and work experiences.
  • I’m also a big fan of traveling and nature, especially quiet, unspoiled places near the ocean – nothing beats the peace of a beautiful natural view.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths:

  • I’m naturally curious and always eager to explore and understand the world around me.
  • When something piques my interest, I dive deep into learning and understanding it wholeheartedly.
  • I’m open, friendly, and enthusiastic in communication, which helps me build meaningful relationships and learn from others.

Weaknesses:

  • Sometimes I can be lazy or easily distracted when working on a specific task.
  • There are moments when I fall into a state I call “virtual effort” – feeling like I’ve done a lot, but in reality, I’ve barely started or lost focus quickly.

Still, I’m well aware of these challenges and working hard every day to improve myself and grow into a better version of who I want to be.
